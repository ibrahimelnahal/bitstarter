This is the landing page for an iphone app that does caffeine tracking. We consume caffee and caffeinated products every day. Unfortunately, there does not exist an easy to use app to track our daily caffeine consumption. There are recommended limits about how much caffeine we can consume every day, and this app makes it easy to track all of this. You can enter which beverage you have consumed and at what time of the day and the app would compute how much caffeine is in your bloodstream.

The app allows one to be conscious of daily coffee consumption habits. The initial version will only track black tea and coffee. We will gradually add other coffeinated beverages, such as latte and cappucino, and then cold caffeinated beverages. Future versions will be able to link the caffeine consumption to sleep quality. We will also add different beverage types from well known coffee shops.
